Hybrid ML-Physics Models¶

This is a list of dynamical models thar are commonly used for research into hybrid ML-physics modeling. The list is in alphabetical order.

Lorenz ‘63¶

  • Reference: Lorenz 1963

  • Implementations: Wikipedia

  • Description: Legendary “butterfly” model with three coupled ODEs that exhibit chaotic behavior.

  • Examples of papers using this models: Scher and Messori 2019

Lorenz ‘96¶

  • Reference: Lorenz 1996

  • Implementations: https://github.com/raspstephan/Lorenz-Online

  • Description: Chaotic model. Often used in its two-layer version for parameterization research.

  • Examples of papers using this models: Rasp 2020, Gagne et al. 2020
